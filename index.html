Transmit data between Chrome and Arduino nano/uno over a USB

The FILLIN.AI team at the leadersofdigital.ru hackathon has developed a program for detecting cheating on online exams. Using modern approaches of artificial intelligence, it recognizes the user, monitors his actions during the exam, and conduct an analysis for cheating. The service determines the substitution of a student during the exam, the direction of gaze, the presence of people nearby, copying text, leaving the mouse outside the browser window. FILLIN.AI is a cloud-based solution, you just need to start your exam in a browser and the service will start analyzing. Webrtc technology is used to transfer data from a web camera and monitor. My task in the team was web development.
